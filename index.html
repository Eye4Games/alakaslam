Face off against rival sorcerers and become a legend in the greatest tennis match of all… the AlakaSLAM!

What is AlakaSlam?

AlakaSLAM is a 2 or 4 player card game: a tennis match, spiced up with magic, and folded up into one small box.

Players manage their hand's resources as they lob the ball back and forth, adding an enchantment to force the opponent to spend even more cards. Just like tennis, points are scored whenever a player cannot return the ball or make it across the net. It's a game of strategizing and wearing your opponent down with your superior footwork and spellwork.
